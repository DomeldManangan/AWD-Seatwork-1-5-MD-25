Who We Are

LegenX Liner is a premier bus transportation company in the Philippines, dedicated to connecting people and places with excellence. We prioritize safety, reliability, and comfort, ensuring every journey is a stress-free and enjoyable experience. With modern, well-equipped buses and professional drivers, we are committed to delivering affordable and accessible travel solutions for passengers across the nation. At LegenX Liner, we don’t just take you to your destination—we take you beyond limits.

Mission

To provide safe, reliable, and comfortable bus transportation that connects people and communities across the Philippines, fostering exceptional travel experiences through innovation, professionalism, and customer-centric service.

Vision

To be the leading bus transportation provider in the Philippines, setting the standard for excellence in service, sustainability, and nationwide connectivity, while empowering communities and promoting seamless mobility for all.

Our story

LegenX Liner was founded by Domeld Manangan with a clear vision: to transform the way Filipinos travel. What began as a simple idea to provide better transportation has grown into a trusted name in the industry. Domeld’s passion for connecting people and communities inspired him to create a company that prioritizes safety, reliability, and comfort for every passenger.

From the start, we’ve been committed to making every journey a memorable experience. With modern, well-equipped buses and professional drivers, we ensure that every trip is smooth, safe, and enjoyable. Over the years, we’ve expanded our routes, making travel more accessible and convenient for countless communities across the Philippines.

At LegenX Liner, we believe that travel is more than just reaching a destination—it’s about creating connections and lasting memories along the way. Guided by Domeld Manangan’s vision, we continue to grow, evolve, and set new standards in the transportation industry. We take pride in being a part of your journey, helping you travel beyond limits.
